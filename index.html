Our Story

Ilitha design is a proudly South African brand that aims on creating crafted design pieces from recycled materials, we pride ourselves in creating functional furniture pieces and appliances that will work for residential spaces. Ilitha means light and light drives out darkness, light can also mean elaborate or share which in our company we aim to teach or share ways in which sustainability can be a daily practice in our communities, to leave this planet in a better place for the next generation.
